There are currenly two types of arrows:
1. Standard Arrows (by pressing 'a')
2. Snake Arrows (by pressing 's')

collapse both of these arrows down to pressing 'a' and when you let go of the arrow, a menu shows up at your cursor letting you select the type of arrow you want.

Additionaly add a new arrow type called 'ghost arrow' that is also in the menu when you create an arrow with 'a'.

This arrow works exactly like a standard and snake arrow when you are "setting" it, meaining the temporary arrow shows up while you are still holding down 'a'.

The difference is that when you let go of 'a' the arrow is not visible like a regular arrow or a snake arrow, rather a small icon is added to the top right corner of both the start and end word of the arrow. And when one of those words is hovered, both of the words are highlighted until the user moves the mouse away from one of the words.

Hovering over either word and pressing the 'delete' key will remove the arrow from both the reading scene and the study overview.
